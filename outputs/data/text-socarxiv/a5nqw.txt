OPEN SCIENCE SINCE COVID-19: OPEN ACCESS + OPEN DATA

The coronavirus crisis has created different initiatives that promote access to open publications and open
data, solve collaboratively and from different places, being an example of the benefits of open science.
From the initial version of the Compilation on Open Science from COVID-19: Open Access + Open Data
(Version I: April 3, 2020) published by Alejandro Uribe-Tirado (http://eprints.rclis.org/39864/), it seemed to
us, a good practice to update this first input openly and collaboratively, using the platform:
https://etherpad.wikimedia.org/p/covid19. This new version (Version II: June 3, 2020), is the result of this
joint work.
